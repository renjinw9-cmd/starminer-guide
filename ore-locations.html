1. How Scanning Works

Press C to activate the scanner. Per Dev Diary #7, asteroid surfaces display colored patches:

Patch ColorResource TypeDescription
🔵 BlueSilicates (Si)Most common scan signal — used for construction and repairs
🟢 GreenPrimary ResourcesIron, Cobalt, Thorium, Eonite, or H₂O on icy asteroids

💡 Asteroid Appearance Also Helps

Asteroids with different resources vary in shape, size, and type. Experienced miners can often identify them before scanning.

2. All 6 Resource Types

Starminer EA currently has 6 harvestable resources (confirmed by Dev Diary #7):

ResourceSymbolSell Price (per unit)Uses
IronFe12 creditsBasic construction material — needed by most modules
SilicatesSi12 creditsConstruction material + the ONLY material usable for repairs
CobaltCo72 creditsAdvanced module construction. Best early-game money maker
ThoriumTh360 creditsAdvanced modules, nuclear reactors. Mid-game economic backbone
Water / IceH₂OLowEssential for colonist survival. Stored in Water Cisterns
EoniteEoVery high (~120/unit)Rare element — the only resource that powers Link Gates for interstellar travel

💰 Money Priority

Cobalt (72c) → Thorium (360c) → Eonite (very high). Do NOT sell Iron and Silicates — you'll need them for construction and repairs. Keep H₂O too — colonists die without water.

3. Refining Tiers

Raw ore must be processed through refineries before it can be used or sold:

RefineryCapacityCan ProcessBest For
T1 Refinery1 chunk / cycleIron, Silicates, CobaltEarly mobile mining ships
T2 Refinery3 chunks / cycleAbove + Thorium⭐ Most important upgrade — unlocks Thorium processing
T3 Refinery5 chunks / cycleAbove + EoniteEndgame stationary sites. Large, high power draw

4. Where to Find Each Resource

ResourceBest LocationDangerNotes

SilicatesAsteroid belts, ring systems, blue scan patches🟢 SafeAbundant but critical — your repairs depend on it

ThoriumC-class asteroids (large, dark, cratered)🟡 Low RiskOccasional scout patrols. Bring a PD turret
H₂O / IceIcy asteroids, outer system ice fields🟢 SafeMore colonists = faster consumption
EoniteDeep space rare asteroids (glowing veins)🔴 High RiskRequires T3 Refinery. Endgame objective
