1. How to Find Ores (Scanner Guide)

Press V to activate the resource scanner overlay. Ore deposits appear as colored blips on your HUD and mini-map. Colors indicate ore tier:

Blip ColorTierOre TypesValue Range
⚪ GreyCommonIron, Silicon, Carbon5-15 credits/unit
🟢 GreenUncommonCopper, Titanium, Aluminum15-40 credits/unit
🔵 BlueRareGold, Platinum, Silver40-100 credits/unit
🟣 PurpleRare EarthNeodymium, Yttrium, Scandium100-250 credits/unit
🟠 OrangeExoticMixed rare earth cluster (highest value!)200-400 credits/unit

💡 Right-Click for Detail Scan

Target an asteroid and right-click to see its exact ore composition before committing a Drill Node. A "rich" deposit has subtle visual cracks and a denser scanner signal.

💰 Rare Earth = Your Income Engine

A single Drill Node on a Neodymium deposit generates ~18,000 credits/hour in refined value. 3 Nodes on rare earth = 54,000/hour. This is how you go from scrapping by to rolling in credits.
