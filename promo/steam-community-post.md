Hey everyone! I've been putting together a comprehensive Starminer guide site since launch, and wanted to share it with the community. It's up to 10 full pages now covering pretty much every system in the game.

**What's covered:**

🚀 **Beginner's Guide** — First hour walkthrough, controls, early priorities, how to stabilize your station before expanding

⛏️ **Mining & Resources** — Complete breakdown of scanners, lasers, haulers, refineries, Drill Nodes, every resource type with values

🔧 **Ship Building Masterclass** — Mass management, thruster placement, power grids, modular design, proven ship blueprints

🔬 **Research & Tech Tree** — Optimal unlock order, RP generation, leveling guide, which research to avoid early

🛡️ **Combat & Defense** — Weapon loadouts, shield setups, heat signature management, enemy counter-tactics, base defense

💰 **Money Making Guide** — Credit economy, best resources to sell, early/mid/late game strategies, debt management

💀 **Survival Mode Guide** — Complete strategy for Starminer's hardest mode. Heat discipline, first hour critical window, base design, death spiral recovery

📦 **Module Database** — Full reference tables: Power (6 modules), Mining (7), Processing (6), Thermal (4), Ship Systems (6), Combat (9), Infrastructure (7). Stats, costs, requirements for every module.

❓ **FAQ (30 Questions)** — Controls, mining, ships, research, combat, economy, bugs, meta — answered concisely

**Why I made this:**
The game has a lot of depth but the in-game tutorials are light. I found myself alt-tabbing constantly to piece together info from different sources. This is my attempt to put everything in one place.

**Link:** https://starminer-guide.vercel.app/

The site is fan-made, not monetized (yet — hoping to get AdSense approved eventually), no signup, no tracking beyond basic analytics. I'm updating it as the game gets new patches.
