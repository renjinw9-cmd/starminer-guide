1. Mining Ship Design Rules

A good mining ship does one thing: extract ore efficiently and survive long enough to deliver it. Follow these rules:

  • Lateral thrusters are mandatory — you need to strafe around asteroids while keeping lasers on target
  • Cooling ≥ 1.5× laser heat — if your laser generates 35 heat/sec, you need at least 52 cooling
  • Cargo capacity determines trip length — more cargo = fewer trips = more efficient. But more mass = slower
  • Always carry a PD turret — miners are targets. A single PD turret stops most drone harassment
  • No heavy weapons — a mining ship with a plasma cannon is wasting mass. Fight or mine, don't do both
