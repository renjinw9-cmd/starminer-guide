What Is Starminer?

A deep-space industrial mining simulator

Starminer is an open-world space simulation sandbox developed by CoolAndGoodGames. Launched into Steam Early Access on May 27, 2026, it puts you in command of a mining operation in deep space.

The game features:

  • Newtonian physics flight — 6DOF movement with realistic mass and thrust mechanics
  • Deep mining system — scan, drill, haul, and refine resources across multiple star systems
  • Modular ship & station building — design everything from small mining drones to massive orbital stations
  • Full automation chains — Drill Nodes, haulers, refineries, and logistics terminals
  • Tech tree progression — research new modules, weapons, and capabilities
  • Heat & energy management — balance power generation, thermal output, and alien threat levels
  • Campaign, Survival, and Sandbox modes — play your way

💡 Pro Tip

Start in Campaign mode. It introduces systems gradually through missions. Sandbox gives more freedom but zero guidance — save it for when you know the mechanics.
