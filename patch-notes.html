1. EA Launch — May 27, 2026 (v0.33)

Starminer launched into Steam Early Access developed by CoolAndGoodGames. Launch content included:

  • Game modes: Campaign, Sandbox, Survival, Tutorial scenarios, Benchmark mode
  • Core systems: 6DOF Newtonian flight, mining, ship building, research, combat
  • Modular building: socket-based system with T1/T2 attachment points, Hulk Node structural backbone
  • Resources: Iron (Fe), Silicon (Si), Cobalt (Co), Thorium (Th), Eonite (Eo), Water (H₂O)
  • DLSS Super Resolution, Frame Generation, and ray tracing support
  • Launch discount: 10% off until June 10

Launch reception: Mostly Positive on Steam (1,100+ reviews, ~74% positive).

6. What's Next

The developers have stated the Early Access phase is expected to last approximately one year. An official development roadmap has not yet been published, but the dev team has indicated one will be shared after the initial launch stabilization period.

Known areas of active development:

  • Additional star systems and content
  • More enemy variety
  • Performance optimization and bug fixes (ongoing)
  • Community-requested features via in-game feedback tool

The developers are actively patching — 4 updates in the first 11 days of Early Access — and responding to community feedback through the in-game reporting system and official Discord.
