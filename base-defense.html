1. Why Stations Get Attacked

Stations are heat magnets. Refineries, reactors, and cargo transfers generate constant thermal output that aliens detect from 10-15km away. Unlike ships, stations can't run or dodge. If your station's combined heat signature exceeds 60%, expect a raid within 90 seconds.

⚠️ The #1 Station Killer

Players build massive industrial stations with 3 refineries and no turrets, then wonder why a Frigate destroyed 200,000 credits of infrastructure. The rule is: 1 turret per heat-generating module, minimum.

2. Turret Placement & Coverage

Turrets have a firing arc — they can't shoot through your own station. Proper placement ensures 360° coverage with no blind spots.

💡 Identify the Threat Vector

Aliens always approach from the nearest hostile zone marker on your map. Identify which direction that is and place 70% of your turrets facing that way. The remaining 30% cover flanks and rear in case of encirclement.

3. Shield Strategy for Stations

Station shields work differently from ship shields — they project a spherical bubble around the entire station, covering all attached modules. Larger stations need stronger shields to maintain bubble integrity.

  • Light Shield: Covers up to 10 modules. 2,000 HP. Regen: 50/sec. Good for starter stations.
  • Medium Shield: Covers up to 25 modules. 5,000 HP. Regen: 120/sec. Standard for mid-game stations.
  • Heavy/Capital Shield: Covers up to 50 modules. 12,000 HP. Regen: 300/sec. Required for fortress stations.
  • Redundancy: Install a backup shield generator. If the primary goes down during a raid, the secondary auto-activates.

4. Defense Platform Designs

PD Platform (Anti-Drone/Swarm): Small Reactor + 3× PD Turret Mk II + Battery + Basic Fin. 8 modules. Cost: ~12,000. Place 3-5 around your station at 2km distance. They create a drone kill-zone before enemies reach your main station.

Heavy Defense Platform (Anti-Corvette/Frigate): Medium Reactor + 2× Plasma Turret + 1× Railgun Turret + Medium Shield + 3× PD Turret + Cryo-Chamber ×2. 15 modules. Cost: ~45,000. Place 2-3 between your station and hostile zones. These will kill or severely damage frigates before they reach firing range.

5. Raid Response Protocol

  1. Detection: Your station's threat warning system alerts you when hostile signatures appear within 10km
  2. Immediate action: Shut down non-essential industrial modules (refineries, excess reactors) — this drops heat and may cause weaker enemies to lose lock
  3. Power to shields: Press [2] on any docked ship or station control to maximize shield regen
  4. Launch combat ships: If you have patrol ships, launch them immediately. Intercept enemies 5km+ from the station
  5. Target priority: Scouts first (prevent reinforcements) → Corvettes (immediate threat) → Frigates (sustained damage)
  6. Repair immediately: After the raid, use the Repair Bay to fix all damage. Don't wait — the next raid could come in 5 minutes
